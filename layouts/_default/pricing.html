FAQ

Short answers, up front

Do I need to pay to use Qooee Contacts?

No. The core product is free to use.

Can I import my existing contacts for free?

Yes. Importing contacts is included in the free plan.

Can I export my data if I stop using Qooee?

Yes. Free includes export in standard formats.

What does pro add?

Pro adds more control over privacy, multiple cards or profiles, fuller history, and advanced native contacts sync with more control over merging and visibility.
