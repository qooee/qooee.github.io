The problem

Contact details should not go stale

Most contact tools store information. They do not help maintain it. People change roles, phone numbers, email addresses, and locations. Details drift. Address books decay. Staying in touch becomes admin.

Manual upkeep

Updating contacts by hand is slow and easy to ignore.

Professional drift

People change jobs and details more often than address books can keep up.

Too much noise elsewhere

Social networks mix contact utility with feeds, performance, and distraction.
